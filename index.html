About us



RBHV E/M High School is located in Mancherial, Adilabad. It is a State Board school, with a team of 13 dedicated and professional faculties, which are here to ensure that the children get the most from their education. In the year 1986 RBHV E/M High School being launched. English is the primary medium of instruction for this school and the student teacher ratio is 38:1. The school tries to provide best learning environment.
Classes from 1 to 10 run in this school. 499 is approximately the current student strength of this school. The library in this school has 3165 books. The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too. This school has 100% of students qualifying the examination and out of these 100% scored first grade.
